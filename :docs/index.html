Neuro-Symbolic AI for Off-Target Toxicity Analysis

Understanding hidden drug risks through knowledge graphs and AI

Why This Matters

Many drugs cause unexpected side effects due to off-target protein interactions. We built a knowledge graph combining DrugBank, ChEMBL, GO, Open Targets, and FAERS to reason about toxicity pathways using neuro-symbolic methods.
